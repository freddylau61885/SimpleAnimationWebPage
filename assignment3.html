Japanese Sword - Katana

Japanese sword also call Katana in Japanese. The curved Bronze blades made after Heian Period(794-1185) are most people acknowledge as the Japanese swords today. However, These are just one of the types of Japanese swords. Swords can be differ by shape, size, application and method of manufacture. In the following, we will talk about three common-known types of swords that are Katana, Tachi, and Nodachi.
